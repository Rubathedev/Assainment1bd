Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh excels in innovation through fintech (bKash), ride-sharing (Pathao), microfinance (Grameen Bank), sustainable farming (flood-resistant rice), green technology (solar power, Sonali Bags), digital governance (A2I Program), and global RMG exports.

Sustainability

Bangladesh focuses on sustainability through solar energy (IDCOL), eco-friendly jute products (Sonali Bags), green factories in RMG, waste management, and climate-resilient agriculture (flood and saline-tolerant crops).

infrastructure

Bangladesh's infrastructure includes major projects like the Padma Bridge, Dhaka Metro Rail, deep-sea ports (Payra, Matarbari), and highways. It focuses on power plants, urbanization, and ICT infrastructure under the "Digital Bangladesh" initiative.

Empowerment

Bangladesh empowers its people through microfinance (Grameen Bank, BRAC), women’s education and employment programs, mobile financial services (bKash), community healthcare, and skill development initiatives like "Digital Bangladesh..

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development

Digital transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
